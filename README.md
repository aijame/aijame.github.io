# AIJAN
AI Journal of Academic Nonsense

# 🌍 Focus and Scope

AIJAN focuses on AI’s impact on humanity, not just its technical brilliance.
We particularly welcome reflections, research, and essays on:

AI adaptation in education systems

AI and mental health

Ethics, economy, and social consequences of automation

AI and academic freedom

Technological displacement, digital inequality, and cognitive overload

Academic culture, gatekeeping, and reform
