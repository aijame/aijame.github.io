AIJAM is inspired by the history of the University of Cambridge establishment in 1209, when a group of scholars left University of Oxford following a dispute for political academic nonsense and settled in the town of Cambridge. AIJAM believe in metascience research impacted by AI and digital arts shaping & changing human behaviors and interactions; even studying and tracking data of scientists' nonsense behaviors such as academic bullying, wrong academic policies, researchers' financial struggling, & mental health.
