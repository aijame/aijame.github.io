# 🌍 Focus and Scope

AIJAN focuses on AI’s impact on humanity, not just its technical brilliance.
We particularly welcome reflections, research, and essays on:

AI Advancements and AI in Computational Science Domains

AI adaptation on economy, and social consequences 

Technological displacement, digital inequality, and cognitive overload

Mechanical Computers, Transponder AI systems, AI systems

AI for Tracing Academic Bullying

AI for Computational Neuroscience and Computational Mental Health
